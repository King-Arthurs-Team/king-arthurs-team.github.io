## About the Game  
Valheim is a cooperative Viking survival game set in a procedurally generated world.  
You explore biomes, defeat bosses, build longhouses, and craft powerful equipment in a myth-inspired environment.

If you want to play on our server, feel free to contact us and we’ll bring the world online for you!
